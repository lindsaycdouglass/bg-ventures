Founded in 2011, BGV is a real estate private equity firm company with offices in Phoenix, AZ and the Washington, DC MSA that pursues real estate investment and development opportunities as well as selective debt investments.

“Institutional training and background within an entrepreneurial firm.”

The principals have been instrumental in leading some of the most well regarded real estate investment and development firms in the country including JMB, JER, Westcor, and GEM Realty. The BGV team’s experience spans nearly all commercial real estate property types and all levels of the capital structure.
